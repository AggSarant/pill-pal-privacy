# Privacy Policy for Pill Pal

**Last updated:** May 15, 2026

Pill Pal ("the app", "we", "us") is a medication reminder app for personal use. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app handles, how, and your rights regarding that information.

**Developer:** Aggelos Sarantopoulos
**Contact email:** aggelos.sarantopoulos@gmail.com

---

## 1. Data stored on your device only

Most data you enter into Pill Pal stays on your device. It is **never** transmitted to us, our servers, or any third party:

- Pill names, schedules, days, and times
- Whether you've taken each pill today
- Your streak and completion history
- Your name (if you enter one in Settings)
- Family contacts (names and phone numbers you add for "Family Notification")
- Your chosen language

This data is stored in the app's local storage on your device only. We do not operate any server that receives or stores this information.

The single exception is that family-contact phone numbers and the check-in message are passed to **WhatsApp** or **Viber** at the exact moment you tap "Send" — and only to the messaging app you chose. Those apps have their own privacy policies and we have no control over how they handle the information from that point on.

You can delete everything Pill Pal has stored on your device at any time via **Settings → Delete all my data**, or by uninstalling the app.

---

## 2. Data collected by Google AdMob (advertising)

Pill Pal displays advertising provided by Google AdMob. AdMob and Google may collect:

- Your Android Advertising ID (AAID)
- Approximate device information (model, OS version, screen size)
- IP address (used to determine general geographic location)
- Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks, time spent)

This data is collected and processed by Google according to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) and [Google AdMob's terms](https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543). We do not see or store this data ourselves.

### Personalized ads and consent

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, Pill Pal shows a consent dialog on first launch (via Google's User Messaging Platform), asking whether you agree to personalized advertising. If you decline, or are not in those regions, you may still see non-personalized ads (based on the current context only, not your behavior).

You can change your choice at any time via **Settings → Manage privacy choices**.

---

## 3. Data automatically collected by Google Play Services

Android devices include Google Play Services, which may collect crash diagnostics and basic device information as part of normal operating-system behavior. Pill Pal does not have access to or control over this data. See [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

---

## 4. Notifications

Pill Pal schedules local notifications on your device using Android's `AlarmManager`. These reminders are entirely on-device — Pill Pal does **not** use any push-notification servers or send any data to us when an alarm fires.

---

## 5. Children

Pill Pal is intended for adults, particularly older adults managing daily medication. It is not designed for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

---

## 6. Your rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you have the following rights regarding personal data processed about you:

- **Right of access** — request a copy of any personal data we hold about you.
- **Right to rectification** — request correction of inaccurate data.
- **Right to erasure** — delete your data. Use **Settings → Delete all my data**, or contact us.
- **Right to restrict processing.**
- **Right to data portability.**
- **Right to object** to processing — including to advertising, via **Settings → Manage privacy choices**.
- **Right to withdraw consent** at any time, where processing is based on consent.
- **Right to lodge a complaint** with your national data-protection authority.

Because virtually all your data is stored only on your device, you can exercise most of these rights directly through the app's Settings. For anything else, contact us at the email above and we will respond within 30 days.

---

## 7. Data retention

- **On-device data:** kept until you delete it via Settings or uninstall the app.
- **AdMob advertising data:** retained by Google according to their policies, typically up to 14 months for advertising data.
